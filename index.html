ColPali Research Engine

A containerized Vision-RAG pipeline optimized for the NVIDIA Blackwell architecture. It bypasses traditional OCR to perform Late Interaction over document images directly.

This engine performs visual similarity searches across indexed PDF libraries, specifically targeting complex tables, charts, and diagrams that standard text-based RAG systems often miss.
